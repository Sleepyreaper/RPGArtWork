# Brief 001 — Portrait: Morwen

**Asset:** `portrait_morwen.png`
**Canvas:** 192 × 48 px (4 frames in a row, each 48 × 48)
**Frame order:** Neutral, Happy, Sad, Angry
**Format:** PNG with alpha + `.procreate` source
**Style ref:** Suikoden II portraits / FF6 dialog portraits — soft pixel
painting, expressive eyes, recognizable at 48 px.

## Character

Morwen, 19, the protagonist. Carries grief from before the game starts;
she rarely smiles in early Act 1 but isn't broken.

- **Hair:** soft brown, just past shoulder length, slightly wavy. Loose
  strand falls near the right eye.
- **Eyes:** hazel-green. The most expressive feature — readable across
  all four emotions.
- **Skin:** warm pale, faint freckles across the nose.
- **Clothes (visible at portrait crop):** cream linen tunic with simple
  embroidery at the collar (Morwen made it herself). Worn dark green
  cloak over one shoulder. Small wooden talisman pendant on a leather
  cord — half-visible at the bottom of the frame.
- **Posture:** head slightly turned 3/4 to her left (camera's right),
  facing forward.

## Emotion breakdown

| Frame | Emotion | What sells it |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Neutral** | Default. Slight downturn at the corners of the mouth — not sad, just quiet. Eyes soft but steady. |
| 2 | **Happy** | Closed-eye half-smile, head tilted slightly. Subtle blush. Not toothy — it's a small, careful happiness. |
| 3 | **Sad** | Eyes lowered, looking off-frame. Mouth a soft line. Faintest highlight of tears at the lower eyelash line — visible but not melodramatic. |
| 4 | **Angry** | Brows lowered, eyes sharp and forward. Mouth set firm. Not snarling — Morwen's anger is controlled and intimidating. |

## Color targets

Soft warm earth palette:

- Skin: `#f5dac4` mid, `#d4a888` shadow, `#fce7d4` highlight
- Cloak: `#3d5a3d` mid, `#2a3f2a` shadow, `#557555` rim
- Tunic: `#e8dcc0` mid, `#b8ac90` shadow

You can deviate ±20% on any of these but stay in the warm/cool harmony.
