# Brief 003 — Portrait: Eira

**Asset:** `portrait_eira.png`
**Canvas:** 192 × 48 px (4 frames in a row, each 48 × 48)
**Frame order:** Neutral, Happy, Sad, Angry
**Format:** PNG with alpha + `.procreate` source

## Character

Eira, ~18 (appearance — actual age ambiguous, she's spirit-touched). A
healer recruited at the Spring Shrine. Gentle but resolute; speaks in
careful, measured words.

- **Hair:** pure white, long (just-past-shoulder), straight with a slight
  inward curl at the ends. NOT old-person-white — *spirit*-white, with
  the faintest silver-blue undertone in the shadows.
- **Eyes:** pale silver-grey. Slightly luminous (a single pixel
  highlight that makes them look not-quite-human).
- **Skin:** very pale, almost moonlit. Cool undertone.
- **Clothes (visible at portrait crop):** silver-trimmed soft green robe
  collar, V-cut. A circlet on her forehead — small, three silver leaves
  in a row, no jewels. Subtle white embroidery on the collar shows tiny
  spring-leaf motifs.
- **Posture:** head straight, slight forward tilt. Gaze direct but
  unthreatening.

## Emotion breakdown

| Frame | Emotion | What sells it |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Neutral** | Calm, almost ethereal. Eyes meet the viewer's. Mouth a soft, level line. Stillness is her default. |
| 2 | **Happy** | A small smile — closed-mouth but warmer than Morwen's. Eyes brighten visibly; a single pixel of extra light catches them. |
| 3 | **Sad** | Eyes lowered, but only slightly. Shoulders drop. A single tear traces from the corner of her right eye — Eira cries gracefully. |
| 4 | **Angry** | Rare for Eira — and therefore frightening. Brows DOWN sharply, eyes sharp and luminous (bump the eye highlights brighter). Mouth a hard line. The whole face cools 5% — desaturate slightly to show the chill. |

## Color targets

- Hair: `#e8e8f0` base, `#b8b8cc` shadow, `#ffffff` highlight (the
  silver-blue undertone is in the shadow, not the base)
- Skin: `#ece0e0` mid, `#c4b4b4` shadow, `#fff0ec` highlight
- Eyes: `#a8b4c0` iris mid, `#dde4ec` highlight, single white pixel for
  the "not-quite-human" gleam
- Robe collar (green): `#86a886` mid, `#5a7a5a` shadow, `#b0d0b0` rim
- Silver trim: `#c0c8d0` mid, `#9098a0` shadow, `#ffffff` highlight
- Circlet: same silver

## Notes

- Eira's color palette is COOL — counterbalance to Cael's warm and
  Morwen's neutral.
- The "spirit-touched" feel matters more than literal supernatural
  effects. Don't add glow or particle effects. The hint comes from her
  unnaturally still posture and luminous eyes.
- She is not a stoic-cold archetype — she feels deeply, she's just quiet
  about it. The angry frame should be unsettling because we know it
  isn't a pose.
