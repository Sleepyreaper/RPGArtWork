# Brief 008 — Boss: The Blooming Dread

**Asset:** `boss_blooming_dread.png`
**Canvas:** 128 × 128 px (battle pose, PNG + alpha)
**Format:** PNG + `.procreate` source
**Facing:** LEFT (player party on right)

## What it is

The Act 1 final boss. The corpse of the Spring Shrine's GREAT ELDER
TREE — the source-of-life for the entire region — has been infested by
the Wicked Moon. What's now growing where it died is a massive corrupt
flower-creature with the tree's roots as a body.

## Description

- **Form:** Centered, towering. A massive bloomed flower (24-petal
  layered rose-like structure) sits atop a writhing tangle of
  root-tendrils that form the body. The flower is the size of a
  small house — picture a 6-foot rose bloom on tree-thick roots.
- **The flower itself:**
  - Petals are deep crimson on the outer ring, becoming pale pink-cream
    at the center.
  - At the very center is a glowing eye — fully bloomed, fully open,
    pupil tracking the player. Pale spring-green iris (echo of the
    wraith), crimson sclera (the corruption).
  - Streams of black sap drip from the flower edges in heavy drops.
- **Roots/body:**
  - Massive twisted wooden tendrils, ash-grey wood interspersed with
    crimson pulsing veins. The veins should look *alive*.
  - Several tendrils reach toward the player at different heights —
    suggesting the boss's many attack vectors.
- **Posture:** Imposing. Petals fully open like a face turned to face
  the party. Roots planted, slightly forward-leaning.

## Color palette

This is the boss — go bigger on contrast than the regular enemies:

- Petals outer crimson: `#9c1820` mid, `#5a0810` shadow, `#d83838`
  highlight
- Petals inner cream: `#e8c8b8` mid, `#a89488` shadow, `#fce8d4`
  highlight
- Center eye green: `#80c060` saturated mid, `#a8e088` glow highlight,
  `#5a8848` shadow
- Eye crimson sclera: `#a02028` mid, `#7c1018` shadow
- Root wood: `#6c5848` mid, `#3c2c20` shadow, `#9c8068` lit edges (cool
  ash-grey-brown)
- Crimson pulsing veins: `#c02020` mid, `#f04848` brighter on the
  "pulse-light" sections
- Black sap drips: `#1a1010` near-black with faint crimson rim

## Notes

- This boss is the END of Act 1 and the player's first real climactic
  encounter. The visual needs to deliver — when the camera reveals it,
  the player should *feel* the weight.
- The CENTRAL EYE is the key feature. Make sure it's framed by the
  petals so it reads as the focal point. The player will be looking at
  that eye for the entire fight.
- Story note for context (will inform the pose): this thing is what
  the Shrine became when its Elder Tree died. It's not malicious so
  much as DESPERATELY HUNGRY for the life-magic the Shrine used to
  channel. Show that hunger in the pose — the petals fanning open like
  a mouth.
- This piece is also a strong candidate for the eventual Steam
  capsule / marketing — make it portfolio-worthy.
