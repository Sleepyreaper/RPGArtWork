# Brief 002 — Portrait: Cael

**Asset:** `portrait_cael.png`
**Canvas:** 192 × 48 px (4 frames in a row, each 48 × 48)
**Frame order:** Neutral, Happy, Sad, Angry
**Format:** PNG with alpha + `.procreate` source

## Character

Cael, 21, Morwen's childhood friend. A knight in training — earnest,
protective, optimistic in a way that contrasts Morwen's gravity. Newly
recruited from the village garrison.

- **Hair:** reddish-brown, short, slightly messy on top. Bangs swept to
  the left.
- **Eyes:** warm brown, wide-set, expressive.
- **Skin:** sun-warmed tan, faint scar across the right eyebrow (small,
  one diagonal pixel).
- **Clothes (visible at portrait crop):** dark brown leather jerkin over
  a chainmail shirt (the chain edges show at the collar). Russet-red
  cloth scarf knotted at the throat — his village colors.
- **Posture:** facing forward, chin lifted slightly. Knightly bearing
  without being stiff.

## Emotion breakdown

| Frame | Emotion | What sells it |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Neutral** | Slight half-smile by default. Cael wears optimism on his face. Confident but not smug. |
| 2 | **Happy** | Open grin, eyes crinkling at the corners. The "I knew you'd come around" expression. |
| 3 | **Sad** | Brows drawn together with concern, mouth a firm line. Looking forward, not away — Cael grieves *with* you, not alone. |
| 4 | **Angry** | Brows tight, jaw clenched, eyes burning. He gets red-faced when angry — show it in the cheek tone. |
